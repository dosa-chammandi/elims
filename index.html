About ELIMS

A Legacy of Excellence in Pharmaceutical Education

ELIMS College of Pharmacy was established in 2016 in Thrissur with a campus of pleasant environment and the aim of providing quality education in pharmaceutical sciences.

The college operates in a spacious, well-equipped building with hostel, library, and sports facilities. Research programmes are in progress across Pharmaceutics, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmacognosy, Pharmacology, Pharmacy Practice, Quality Assurance, and Biotechnology.

Academics

Programs We Offer

Comprehensive pharmaceutical education designed to produce industry-ready professionals and researchers.

4 Years • 100 Intake

B. Pharm

An intensive full-time programme with experimental learning, workshops, seminars, and hands-on experience guided by the KUHS syllabus.

Learn More →
2 Years

D. Pharm

Diploma in Pharmacy — a foundational course in pharmaceutical sciences preparing graduates for a career in community and hospital pharmacy.

Learn More →
6 Years (5 + 1 Internship)

Pharm.D

Doctor of Pharmacy — an integrated clinical pharmacy program with hands-on hospital training through MOU with Daya General Hospital, Thrissur.

Learn More →
3 Years • 10 Intake

Pharm.D (Post Baccalaureate)

A 3-year bridge program for B.Pharm graduates to earn the Doctor of Pharmacy degree with 1-year clinical internship at Daya General Hospital.

Learn More →
2 Years • 15 Intake

M.Pharm Pharmaceutics

A postgraduate programme in Pharmaceutics under KUHS, as per PCI 2014 regulations, with advanced research and formulation training.

Learn More →
2 Years • 15 Intake

M.Pharm Pharmacy Practice

A clinically oriented postgraduate programme with patient-care postings and evidence-based pharmacy practice training.
