College Profile

ELIMS College of Pharmacy

ELIMS College of Pharmacy was established in 2016 in Thrissur with a campus of pleasant environment with the aim of providing quality education in pharmaceutical sciences. ELIMS College of Pharmacy is run in a spacious well-equipped building of its own with hostel, library and sports facilities.

The pharmacy curriculum is approved by Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Kerala and Kerala University of Health Sciences (KUHS). The college is run by the New Elims Charitable and Educational Trust. Other educational institutions under the ELIMS Group of Institutions are Elijah Institute of Management Studies and ELIMS College of Arts and Science.

Research programmes are in progress in the disciplines of Pharmaceutics, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmacognosy, Pharmacology, Pharmacy Practice, Pharmaceutical Quality Assurance and Pharmaceutical Biotechnology.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Vision

To be a centre of excellence in pharmaceutical education and research, producing competent pharmacists who contribute to the healthcare system and society at large.

Mission

  • To provide quality pharmaceutical education that meets national and international standards.
  • To foster a culture of research and innovation in pharmaceutical sciences.
  • To develop competent pharmacy professionals with ethical values and social responsibility.
  • To establish strong industry-academia partnerships for experiential learning.
  • To contribute to the healthcare needs of the community through pharmacy practice services.
