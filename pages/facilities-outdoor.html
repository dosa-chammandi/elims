Health & Fitness

Sports & Outdoor Activities

ELIMS College of Pharmacy recognizes the importance of physical fitness and a healthy lifestyle in the overall development of students. The college provides excellent outdoor sports facilities and encourages active participation in various sports and recreational activities to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Students are encouraged to participate in inter-collegiate sports events, university-level competitions, and annual sports day celebrations. The college believes that a sound mind in a sound body is essential for academic excellence and professional growth.

Sports & Recreation

⚽ Outdoor Sports

Open grounds for football, cricket, volleyball, badminton, and other outdoor team sports.

🏃 Athletics

Facilities for track and field events, jogging, and general fitness activities on campus.

🏆 Competitions

Active participation in inter-collegiate tournaments, university-level sports meets, and zonal competitions.

🎯 Indoor Games

Indoor recreation facilities including table tennis, chess, carrom, and other board games.

Activities & Events

  • Annual sports day with track and field events for all students
  • Inter-class and inter-department sports tournaments
  • Participation in inter-collegiate and university sports competitions
  • Yoga and fitness sessions for physical and mental well-being
  • Cultural and recreational events promoting holistic development
  • Nature walks and trekking activities organized by the college
  • Sports equipment and kits provided by the institution
