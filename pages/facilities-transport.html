Commute & Connectivity

Transportation

ELIMS provides college bus services from various destinations in Thrissur district to the campus at nominal fees. Apart from this, as the college is situated close to the main road, frequent public transportation is also available to students to commute to and from the campus to Thrissur town.

Transport Facilities

🚌 College Bus Service

Dedicated college buses operating on multiple routes covering various destinations across Thrissur district at nominal fees.

🚏 Public Transport Access

Campus located close to the main road with frequent public bus services connecting to Thrissur town and surrounding areas.

💰 Affordable Fees

College bus services are offered at nominal fees, making daily commuting affordable for all students.

📍 Convenient Location

The campus at Ramavarmapuram, Villadam is well-connected to Thrissur city, making it easily accessible from multiple directions.

Key Highlights

  • Multiple bus routes covering major areas of Thrissur district
  • Safe and well-maintained college buses with experienced drivers
  • Nominal transport fees with flexible payment options
  • Proximity to main road ensures easy access to public buses and auto-rickshaws
  • Well-connected to Thrissur Railway Station and KSRTC Bus Stand
  • Ample parking space available on campus for students with personal vehicles

Campus Location

ELIMS College of Pharmacy is located at Ramavarmapuram P O, Villadam, Thrissur — 680631, Kerala. The campus enjoys excellent road connectivity and is easily accessible from all parts of Thrissur district.
