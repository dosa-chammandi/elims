Academic Departments

Our Departments

ELIMS College of Pharmacy has well-established departments staffed by qualified faculty members.

💊

Pharmaceutics

Focuses on the design, formulation, and manufacturing of drug delivery systems and dosage forms.

⚗️

Pharmaceutical Chemistry

Deals with the design, synthesis, and analysis of pharmaceutical compounds and drug molecules.

🌿

Pharmacognosy

Studies natural drug substances derived from plants, animals, and other biological sources.

🧬

Pharmacology

Explores the mechanisms of drug action, their effects on biological systems, and therapeutic applications.

🏥

Pharmacy Practice

Emphasizes clinical pharmacy skills, patient care, drug information services, and hospital pharmacy management.

✅

Pharmaceutical Quality Assurance

Ensures pharmaceutical products meet established quality standards through rigorous testing and compliance.

For detailed information about each department, please contact the college office.

ELIMS College of Pharmacy

Established in 2016, ELIMS College of Pharmacy provides quality pharmaceutical education in Thrissur, approved by PCI, AICTE and affiliated to KUHS.
