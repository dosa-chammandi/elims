Digital Infrastructure

Computer Lab

ELIMS College of Pharmacy maintains a well-equipped computer lab with modern PCs and high-speed internet access to support the academic and research needs of students and faculty. The lab serves as a vital resource for computer-aided learning, online research, data analysis, and project work.

Students have access to a wide range of software tools commonly used in pharmaceutical sciences, including molecular modelling software, statistical analysis packages, and drug design tools. The lab is available to students during extended hours to ensure maximum utilization.

Lab Features

🖥️ Modern Hardware

Configured PCs with latest operating systems, adequate RAM, and processing power for academic applications.

🌐 High-Speed Internet

Dedicated broadband internet connection for online research, e-learning, and access to digital databases.

📊 Academic Software

Licensed software for molecular modelling, statistical analysis, drug design, and other pharmaceutical applications.

🖨️ Printing & Scanning

Printing and scanning facilities available for academic assignments, reports, and project documentation.

Facilities Available

  • Sufficient number of computers to accommodate practical sessions
  • High-speed broadband internet with Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Licensed software for pharmaceutical and scientific computing
  • Printer, scanner, and photocopier for academic use
  • Ergonomic seating and well-lit environment
  • UPS backup for uninterrupted power supply
  • Extended lab hours for students to complete projects and assignments
