National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF)

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) was approved by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, and launched in 2015. NIRF outlines a methodology to rank institutions across the country based on parameters agreed upon by a core committee set up by the Ministry.

ELIMS College of Pharmacy is proud to be recognized under the NIRF ranking framework, which evaluates institutions based on Teaching, Learning and Resources, Research and Professional Practice, Graduation Outcomes, Outreach and Inclusivity, and Peer Perception.

This recognition underscores our commitment to maintaining the highest standards in pharmaceutical education and research.

NIRF Ranking Parameters

Parameter Description
Teaching, Learning & Resources (TLR) Student strength, faculty-student ratio, faculty qualifications, financial resources, and utilization
Research & Professional Practice (RP) Publications, patents, sponsored projects, and consultancy
Graduation Outcomes (GO) University examinations, median salary, higher studies, and entrepreneurship
Outreach & Inclusivity (OI) Regional diversity, women diversity, economically and socially challenged students, facilities for physically challenged
Perception (PR) Peer perception by academic peers, employers, and competent authorities
