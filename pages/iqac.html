IQAC

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) is a significant administrative body of the institution established to develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic improvement in the overall performance of the institution.

IQAC channelizes efforts and measures of the institution towards academic excellence and works as a driving force for quality culture in the institution. It ensures timely, efficient and progressive performance of academic, administrative and financial tasks.

Objectives & Functions

  • Development of quality benchmarks for the various academic and administrative activities of the institution
  • Documentation of various programs and activities leading to quality improvement
  • Acting as a nodal agency for quality-related activities of the institution
  • Preparation of the Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) as per NAAC guidelines
  • Organizing workshops, seminars, and conferences on quality-related themes

Key Activities

📊 Academic Audits

Regular internal academic audits to ensure curriculum delivery meets quality standards.

📝 Feedback System

Structured feedback collection from students, parents, alumni, and employers for continuous improvement.

🎓 Faculty Development

Organizing faculty development programs, workshops, and training sessions to enhance teaching quality.

📈 Performance Review

Periodic review of institutional performance and implementation of improvement strategies.
