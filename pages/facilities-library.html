Library

The institution has a well-equipped library to support the students in academic and research activities. The library has a collection of the latest books and subscribes to a good number of International Journals. International online databases like Pharmaceutical Abstracts, Scopus, and Science Citation Index journals are available. Wi-Fi enabled and a digital library is an additional facility.

Library Features

  • Extensive collection of latest pharmacy textbooks and reference materials
  • Subscription to national and international pharmaceutical journals
  • Access to online databases — Pharmaceutical Abstracts, Scopus, Science Citation Index
  • Fully functional digital library with e-resources
  • Wi-Fi enabled reading areas for online research
  • Separate reading rooms for students and faculty
  • Book bank scheme for economically weaker students
  • Reprographic facility for photocopying and printing

Resources Available

📖 Books & Journals

A comprehensive collection of pharmacy textbooks, reference books, and subscriptions to leading national and international journals.

🌐 Online Databases

Access to Pharmaceutical Abstracts, Scopus, Science Citation Index, and other premier online research databases.

💻 Digital Library

A dedicated digital library section with computer terminals for accessing e-books, e-journals, and online learning resources.

📶 Wi-Fi Access

High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the library for seamless access to online academic resources.
