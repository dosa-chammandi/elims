4-Year Program

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B. Pharm)

Duration: 4 Years (VIII Semesters) Intake: 100 Students Affiliation: KUHS

ELIMS offers an intensive full-time B.Pharm programme with an intake of 100 students. The curriculum is set for a duration of four years (VIII semesters). To meet the present and future demands of the corporate world, ELIMS emphasizes on experiential learning with a student-centered approach.

The college conducts workshops, seminars and interactive sessions by eminent personalities from the industry and academia, and provides hands-on experience in related fields to students. The theoretical foundation is structured as per Kerala University of Health Sciences syllabus and curriculum.

In addition, a number of innovative and practical methods of learning are provided to prepare students to become the most competitive professionals. Co-curricular activities are an essence in the educational system — the teaching and training system includes continuous evaluation, communication skill development, and more.

Program Highlights

📚 KUHS Curriculum

Structured theoretical foundation as per Kerala University of Health Sciences syllabus.

🔬 Practical Learning

State-of-the-art laboratories with hands-on experiential learning approach.

🎤 Expert Sessions

Workshops and seminars by eminent professionals from industry and academia.

📊 Continuous Evaluation

Regular assessments and communication skill development programs.

Eligibility

Candidates who have passed the Higher Secondary Examinations of the Government of Kerala (or examinations recognized as equivalent) with:

  • 50% marks in Biology/Mathematics separately, and
  • 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics put together.

How to Apply

Admission to 50% Merit seats is through the rank list prepared by the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations based on index marks of KEAM. The remaining 50% Management quota is filled by the Kerala State Self Financing Pharmacy College Management Association (KSSPCMA).
