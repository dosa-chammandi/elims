Admission Procedure 2026–2027

Admissions are now open for the academic year 2026–2027. ELIMS College of Pharmacy invites applications from eligible candidates for its undergraduate pharmacy programs. Explore the eligibility criteria and admission process for each program below.

B. Pharm (4 Years)

Eligibility

Candidates who have passed the Higher Secondary Examination (HSE) of the Government of Kerala or examinations recognized as equivalent with 50% marks in Biology/Mathematics separately and 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics put together.

Admission Process

50% Merit seats are filled through the KEAM rank list prepared by the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations. The remaining 50% Management quota seats are filled by the Kerala State Self Financing Pharmacy College Management Association (KSSPCMA).

Pharm D (6 Years)

Eligibility

Candidates who have passed the Higher Secondary Examination (HSE) of the Government of Kerala or examinations recognized as equivalent with 50% marks in Biology/Mathematics separately and 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics put together.

Admission Process

Admission is through the rank list prepared by the Kerala State Self Financing Pharmacy College Management Association (KSSPCMA).

Download Application ↓

D. Pharm (2 Years)

Eligibility

Candidate who has passed HSE (+2) with Physics, Chemistry and Biology or Mathematics as optional subjects.

Admission Process

50% merit seats are filled as per the merit list based on marks obtained in Physics & Chemistry at the qualifying examination. The remaining 50% management seats are filled by the management.
