Parent Teachers Association (PTA)

The Parent Teacher Association (PTA) plays an important role in the smooth functioning of the college. PTA was constituted on 17-6-2022. All parents of the students are members of the association. It is compulsory for a parent/guardian to be a member of the college.

PTA plays a major role in the day-to-day activities of the institute. The Association meets at intervals to discuss various problems relating to the general welfare of the students. The general body of the PTA meets twice every year; one at the beginning of the academic year and the other at the end. Further, PTA meetings are held once a semester following their first internal examinations. Parent-Teacher-Student Meetings are also convened. The Principal and faculty avail these opportunities to interact with parents.

Objective

To work for the welfare of the students and institution and to offer constructive suggestions for the smooth and successful functioning of the college. To promote better participation of the parents in the various programs of the college and to establish better liaison with teachers.
